Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 24, 2026

At IPTVMine Pro, we prioritize the privacy and security of our users. This Privacy Policy describes what information is collected, how it is stored, and your rights when using the IPTVMine Pro mobile and Android TV applications, as well as the IPTVMine website.

1. Personal Information Collection

IPTVMine Pro is designed as a local media player client. We do not require account registration, email sign-ups, or personal identity checks. We do not collect, harvest, transmit, or store any personal information such as names, phone numbers, IP addresses, or device locations on our servers.

2. Local Storage and Database

All application configurations, watch history lists, favorite bookmarks, and custom M3U playlist paths are stored strictly locally on your physical device storage (such as your phone, tablet, or television box memory). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

3. Third-Party Extensions (Vega Scrapers)

IPTVMine Pro includes support for Vega scrapers, which execute JavaScript code in a headless Android WebView environment. When running these custom extensions, your device may communicate directly with third-party servers to fetch playlist directories and video links. These external requests are subject to the privacy policies of the respective third-party providers. We recommend reviewing the sources of any external extensions you import.

4. Cache and Media Logs

Our playback engine uses cache files to optimize streaming speeds. These temporary cache files are managed automatically by the Android system framework (Media3/ExoPlayer) and can be cleared at any time by visiting your Android app management settings.

5. Open Source Transparency

Since IPTVMine Pro is 100% open source, you can audit our source code at any time on GitHub to verify our data processing safety. We welcome audits and contributions from the developer community.
